    Hero

    The website is the prominent area at the top of a webpage, typically featuring a large, eye-catching image or video, a compelling headline, and a call-to-action (CTA) button. It's designed to grab visitors' attention immediately and convey the main message or purpose of the site. This section often includes minimal text and focuses on high-impact visuals and concise messaging to engage users effectively. The design of the hero section plays a crucial role in shaping the first impression of the website.

    About Web Development

    Web development is the process of building and maintaining websites. It encompasses both front-end development, which deals with the visual and interactive aspects of a site using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and back-end development, which involves server-side programming, databases, and application logic. Full-stack development combines both front-end and back-end skills. Web developers use various tools and frameworks to create functional and user-friendly websites.
